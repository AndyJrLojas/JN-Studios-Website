The Founder

Julia
Novikova

Professional Portrait & Event Photographer

I believe that the best photographs aren't forced; they are observed. With an artist's eye and over a decade of experience behind the lens, my approach is calm, detailed, and deeply personal.

My background sets me apart in the industry. Before establishing JN Studios, I spent seven years in Business Development and Franchise Operations, managing a high-volume commercial photography studio. Leading a large team and overseeing thousands of sessions taught me the importance of operational excellence, consistency, and strict quality control.

I bring that same level of discipline and organization to every shoot. Whether I am capturing a high-stakes corporate headshot, a chaotic wedding day, or a candid family moment, I balance artistic intuition with structured execution. My goal is to make the process effortless for you, ensuring the experience is stress-free and the results are timeless.

Based in Mississauga and serving the entire GTA, I specialize in corporate branding, weddings, and family portraiture, helping clients look their absolute best—no matter the occasion.
